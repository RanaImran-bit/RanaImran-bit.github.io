About Me

Background & Research Interests

I am a first-year PhD student at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, where my research focuses on altermagnetism in kagome lattices and the interplay between superconductivity and quantum phase transitions in strongly correlated systems.

My work bridges theoretical and computational physics, combining quantum Monte Carlo simulations, DFT materials modeling, and machine learning methods (Neural ODEs) to tackle open problems in quantum many-body physics.

I completed my M.S. in Physics at Ludong University, where I worked on generalized Langevin dynamics and stochastic processes. Before that, I earned my B.S. from the University of the Punjab, Lahore.

I am actively seeking research collaborations and Postdoctoral opportunities in condensed matter physics, quantum materials, and AI for science.

Research Interests

  • Altermagnetism & novel magnetic phases in quantum materials

Evolution of Magnetic Correlation in the Doped Hubbard Model with Altermagnetic Spin Splitting
Yinlong Li, Rana Imran Mushtaq, Ji Liu, Wing Chi Yu, Xiaosen Yang, Cho-Tung Yip, Ho-Kin Tang
Physical Review B 113, 134443 · Published 28 April 2026
Investigates how spin splitting generated by spin-dependent next-nearest-neighbor hopping t' reshapes the Fermi surface nesting and Van Hove singularities in the two-dimensional square-lattice Hubbard model. Using constrained-path quantum Monte Carlo, the study finds a transition from antiferromagnetic order at half filling to noncollinear spiral order upon doping, ultimately entering a short-range regime where stripe and spiral correlations coexist, highlighting a possible route to realizing spiral correlation in altermagnetic systems for spintronic devices.
PRB arXiv DOI Altermagnetism Hubbard Model Quantum Monte Carlo
Quantum Filtering and Stabilization of Dissipative Quantum Systems via Augmented Neural Ordinary Differential Equations
Shahid Qamar, Rana Imran Mushtaq, Bo Li, Ho-Kin Tang
arXiv Preprint · 2025
Introduces the Augmented Quantum Neural ODE (AQNODE) framework for quantum filtering and real-time feedback control of dissipative qubits, enabling accurate quantum state prediction and parameter inference from partial measurement data.
arXiv DOI Neural ODEs Quantum Control Open Systems
A Matrix Form Solution of the Multi-Dimensional Generalized Langevin Equation in the Quadratic Potential
Rana Imran Mushtaq, Chunyang Wang, Shichao Zhi, Zengxuan Zhao, Jeolous Nyasulu Malamula
Iranian Journal of Physics Research · 2024
Derives an exact matrix form analytical solution to the multi-dimensional generalized Langevin equation with quadratic potentials, providing a framework for studying stochastic processes in higher-dimensional systems under harmonic potentials.
DOI Langevin Dynamics Stochastic Processes Analytical Methods
Theoretical and Numerical Study on the Fluctuation of the Potential Energy Landscape
Shichao Zhi, Zengxuan Zhao, Rana Imran Mushtaq, Pengchen Zhao, Jeolous Malamula Nyasunu, Chunyang Wang
International Journal of Modern Physics B · 2024
Presents a theoretical and numerical investigation of Brownian particle diffusion over a fluctuating meta-stable potential, introducing a Gaussian-based replacement for the inverse harmonic barrier and a new Monte Carlo simulation algorithm.
