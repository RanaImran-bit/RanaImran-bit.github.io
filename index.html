About

Overview of my academic background, research interests, and professional journey.

Background

I am an academic researcher focusing on quantum materials, strongly correlated electrons, and numerical methods. I enjoy building bridges between rigorous theory and practical computation to illuminate emergent phenomena in complex systems.

Interests

Topics include unconventional superconductivity, altermagnetism, Hubbard/t-J models, and machine-learning accelerated simulation. I also explore methodological advances in high-performance computing and scientific visualization.
